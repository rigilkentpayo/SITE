DRINKS AND DESERT

MAJA BLANCA

Maja blanca is a Filipino dessert with a gelatin-like
consistency made primarily from coconut milk.

STRAWBERRY BANANA SMOOTHIE

This strawberry banana smoothie is made from four
simple ingredients - strawberries, banana, milk and yogurt

CASSAVA CAKE

Cassava cake is a traditional Filipino moist cake made
from grated cassava, coconut milk, and condensed milk with a custard layer on top.

CHERRY SMOOTHIE

This easy smoothie recipe is packed full of goodness and flavor!
Frozen dark cherries come together with banana, yogurt, and almond extract for
an unforgettable breakfast treat or snack!

UBE HALAYA

Ube halaya or halayang ube is a Philippine dessert
made from boiled and mashed purple yam.

RASPBERRY SMOOTHIE

This beautiful puree has it all: the best sweet tart flavor,
creamy frosty texture, and stunning brilliant color.

BUKO PANDAN

Buko Pandan is a Filipino cold dessert made of jelly cubes,
young coconut, and sweetened cream infused with pandan or screwpine flavor.

YEMA

Yema is a sweet custard confectionery from the Philippines.
It is made with egg yolks, milk, and sugar. The name yema is from Spanish for "egg yolk".

LEMONADE

Lemonade is a sweetened lemon-flavored beverage.
There are varieties of lemonade found throughout the world.

CREME CARAMEL

Crème caramel, flan, caramel pudding or caramel
custard is a custard dessert with a layer of clear caramel sauce.

TROPICAL SMOOTHIE

This tropical smoothie recipe is a blend of mango,
pineapple, banana and coconut milk, all mixed together to create a creamy and
refreshing drink. The perfect breakfast or snack option that’s loaded with nutrients!

PINEAPPLE CARROT CAKE

Pineapple carrot cake is incredibly moist, full of spice flavor,
and topped with smooth tangy cream cheese frosting. This simple from-scratch
recipe comes together quickly and easily.
